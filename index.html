Hello. I'm Reese.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who prefers the challenge to create intricate and comprehensive designs with a refined aesthetic. While not working, I can be found covered in wood chips handcrafting functional treasures in my workshop. I believe that the pure art of design appears effortless and gleams with a story to tell. I'd love to share my vision to help you create something amazing.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, CA. I remember the lure of our first home computer. I was introduced to tech early in life watching my mother's prototype robots learning to take their first steps in our home. Thus, puzzle-solving skills and critical thinking were heavily encouraged when I was growing up.

My passion is global preservation. I have always been interested in recycling and re-purposing items. I try to incorporate the philosophy of less is more in every aspect of my life. I desire to make a difference and leave a gentle yet powerful footprint on this earth.

Fun fact, in 2000, I learned to care for exotic animals. I hand-raised a pride of lions. It was also when I met one of the biggest loves of my life, Nellie, an African elephant. It was my honor to include them in wildlife conservation seminars to educate people about the perils of habitat encroachment and endangered species.

Let's be brilliant together!
